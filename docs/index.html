Decomposition

The inventory observed series is split into longterm trend, sesonal change and random shifts.

Residual interpretation

The random component does not look like pure white noise. The residual series still shows strong short-run dependence, with lag-1 autocorrelation of 0.93, and the Ljung-Box test rejects a pure-noise process at both 13 and 26 lags (p-values 0.0000 and 0.0000).

The largest leftover disturbances cluster around winter transition weeks rather than appearing evenly spread through the year. Absolute residuals are largest in January, and the most volatile weeks are 3, 2, 4, 16. That suggests the decomposition captures the broad seasonal cycle, but winter weather shocks and storage regime shifts still leave a patterned residual component.
